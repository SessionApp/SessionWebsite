Privacy Policy

We are very delighted that you have shown interest in our mobile app. Data protection is of a particularly high priority for the management of Session. The use of the Session App is possible with the creation of your account. Session has implemented numerous technical and organizational measures to ensure the most complete protection of personal data processed through this app. However, Internet-based data transmissions may in principle have security gaps, so absolute protection may not be guaranteed. For this reason, every data subject is free to transfer personal data to us via alternative means, e.g. by telephone.

Data collected

When you use our services, you choose to give us certain information. Session asks for your location in order to locate you on the map and have a list of nearby spots. However, this location is not collected by Session. We only collect the data explicitly provided by the user when registering: email, username, password and possibly a referral code. To provide a better service to our users (through "Sessions" functionality), the location of the user (a unique GPS coordinate) may also be collected in order to send notifications when a "session" is created (by another user) around this user location.

Cookies

The Internet pages of Session use cookies. Cookies are text files that are stored in a computer system via an Internet browser. Many Internet sites and servers use cookies. Many cookies contain a so-called cookie ID. A cookie ID is a unique identifier of the cookie. It consists of a character string through which Internet pages and servers can be assigned to the specific Internet browser in which the cookie was stored. This allows visited Internet sites and servers to differentiate the individual browser of the data subject from other Internet browsers that contain other cookies. A specific Internet browser can be recognized and identified using the unique cookie ID.Through the use of cookies, Session can provide the users of this website with more user-friendly services that would not be possible without the cookie setting.
